1. In the evening light, a bark resonates,
2. A wagging tail speaks words unspoken,
3. Jumps of joy, playful, never waits,
4. In every moment, love is awoken.
5. Breeds of every hue, and size we see,
6. From tiny paws to giants, they all run free.
7. With eyes that shine like stars above,
8. In their gaze, we find pure love.
9. Best friends they are, through thick and thin,
10. With a gentle nudge, they pull us in.
11. They guard our homes, they heal our hearts,
12. In every ending, they play their parts.
13. From playful pups to aged with grace,
14. In every memory, they leave a trace.
15. In the quiet night, as dreams unfold,
16. The warmth of a dog is a treasure to hold.
