1. In a world of wagging tails, so bright,
2. Beneath the sun, in morning light.
3. With hearts so pure, and spirits free,
4. Dogs bring joy, as we all see.
5. 
6. Loyal friends, through thick and thin,
7. With every bark, they pull us in.
8. In roles they play, both big and small,
9. From service hounds to pets that sprawl.
10. 
11. They dash and leap, with boundless glee,
12. Chasing dreams, wild and carefree.
13. In every game, in every chase,
14. They fill our homes with love’s embrace.
15. 
16. The bonds we share, so deep and true,
17. A silent promise, just us two.
18. Each wag, each glance, a language clear,
19. In their presence, we shed our fear.
20. 
21. So here’s to dogs, in all they do,
22. Our cherished friends, forever true.
23. In every bark, in every sigh,
24. They leave paw prints on our hearts, oh my.
